Founder & Principal

Gil Rogoff

Strategy & Partnerships Leader

Gil is a strategy and partnerships leader with over 15 years of experience helping companies scale through GTM strategy, business development, marketing, and executive leadership.

He has built and led global partnership programs generating millions of dollars in revenue and pipeline across North America, EMEA, and APAC. His work spans Fortune 500 enterprises, high-growth startups, and Web3 protocols.

A builder at heart, Gil has been an early team member at multiple startups — including two ad-tech exits — while also holding leadership roles at Oracle, Ingram Micro/CloudBlue, and WeWork. He has also advised on strategy consulting and digital transformation initiatives with MCL Digital and Roland Berger.

Gil brings a big-picture perspective to every engagement, connecting the dots between product, marketing, partnerships, and revenue to drive sustainable growth.

Gil holds an MBA from ESADE Business School in Barcelona and a BS in Business Marketing from the University of Arizona.

He works with early-stage founders navigating critical inflection points — whether that's landing first enterprise customers, building partnership strategies, or raising capital.
